# Industry Landscape & Parallel Developments

## 1. The Rise of Infrastructure-First Development
The industry is observing a shift from "Application-First" to "Infrastructure-First" development. Key players are focusing on solving the underlying problems of scale, trust, and verification rather than user-facing interfaces.

### 1.1. Key Sectors
*   **Autonomous Agent Economies**: Moving beyond chatbots to agents that can transact and verify contracts.
*   **Network States & Digital Jurisdictions**: Projects exploring mechanisms for digital-first governance and verifiable citizenship.
*   **Verifiable Compute**: Platforms proving that a specific computation was executed correctly without trusting the executor.
